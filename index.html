About The Initiative

The Ataoja Palace Project Initiative (APPI), is created purposely to raise fund for the renovation and modernization of Ataoja's Palace towards the celebration of 10th year anniversary on the throne of His Royal Majesty, Oba Jimoh Olanipekun Abidemi Oyetunji, Larooye II, the current Ataoja of Osogbo Land.
